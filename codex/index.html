Sources and assumptions

This is an educational estimator, not a formal inventory. It uses U.S. customary inputs and reports annual pounds of CO2e. AI and digital-service factors are especially uncertain, so the interface uses editable activity assumptions and frames AI both as an individual-use slice and as a fast-growing system-level electricity issue.
